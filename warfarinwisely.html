What is WarfarinWisely?

WarfarinWisely is a Clinical Decision Support (CDS) tool designed to assist licensed healthcare professionals in calculating modified weekly warfarin doses based on INR (International Normalized Ratio) test results. The app simplifies a well-documented pharmaceutical calculation using validated guidelines from the Pharmaceutical Services Programme, Ministry of Health Malaysia (Anticoagulant Quick Guide, 1st Edition).

This tool is NOT a medical device, NOT medical advice, and NOT a substitute for professional clinical judgment. Healthcare professionals must independently verify all calculated results before clinical application.

Calculation Methods

The app offers three evidence-based adjustment methods for calculating modified warfarin doses:

  • Method A (ICSI Guideline): Percentage-based adjustment (wadj = 15 √ó INR difference %)
  • Method B (KKM Guideline): Alternative percentage-based adjustment (wadj = 10 √ó INR difference %)
  • Method C (Manual/Custom): Custom adjustment defined by the healthcare professional (-15% to +15%)
